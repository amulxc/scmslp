Courses offered & Eligibility Criteria

PGDM FPM
Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

The flagship programme of SCMS Cochin is known for its dynamic curriculum and teaching methodology. The 2-year full-time programme is AICTE approved and has a total intake of 120 seats.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

A candidate is required to have secured a minimum of 50 percent in graduation from a recognized university. Final Year students are eligible to apply. Applicants are also required to have a valid score in CAT/ XAT/ MAT/ CMAT/ KMAT (Kerala)/ ATMA.

Fellow Programme in Management (FPM)

The 4-year FPM programme covers basic management research courses in the first year, while the latter three years are devoted to research and the doctoral dissertation. The FPM programme is approved by AICTE and has an intake of 20 seats.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

A candidate is required to have secured a first class in Master’s programme from a recognized university. Final Year students are eligible to apply. Candidates with 5 years’ work experience are eligible to pursue the programme in the part-time mode.
