Courses offered & Eligibility Criteria

Post Graduate Diploma in Management

The two-year, full-time PGDM is the flagship programme of SCMS Cochin School of Business and is known for its dynamic curriculum and teaching methodology. It is approved by AICTE, accredited by NBA and ACBSP, USA, certified by ISO and recognized by the Association of Indian Universities, as equivalent to MBA.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • 50% aggregate in UG
  • Valid score in CAT/XAT/MAT/CMAT/KMAT(Kerala)/ATMA
  • Valid score in Group Discussion and Personal Interview

Fellow Programme in Management

SCMS Cochin School of Business offers Fellow Programme in Management (FPM), which is a 3-5 year, AICTE approved, full-time/ part-time doctoral research programme in various areas of Management. Designed to cultivate advanced research and teaching skills, it prepares scholars to become expert researchers and educators in the diverse management areas.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • Master's Degree or equivalent in Engineering and Technology/ Management/ Economics/ Social Science/Biological Science/ Pure Science/Commerce/ Humanities with FIRST CLASS
  • To pursue the programme in part time mode, a minimum of 5 years' work experience is mandatory

MBA from Northern Kentucky University, USA

SCMS School of International Studies has partnered with Northern Kentucky University (NKU),USA for a global MBA programme. The first year (14 credits) will be taught at SCMS, Cochin campus, while the second year (19 credits) will take place at NKU, USA campus. This collaboration prepares students for international management careers.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • 75% in Graduation (preferable)
  • IELTS 6.5 / TOEFL iBT 79 (550 paper based)
  • Valid score in Personal Interview

Master of Business Administration

The two-year, full-time MBA programme is offered by SCMS School of Technology and Management. SSTM is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. The programme is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), and is ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring high standards of academic excellence.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • 50% aggregate in UG (score is as per MGU notification if candidate is eligible for reservation)
  • Valid score in CAT/XAT/MAT/CMAT/KMAT(Kerala)/ATMA
  • Valid score in Group Discussion and Personal Interview

Integrated Master of Computer Applications

SCMS School of Technology and Management is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. SSTM has been awarded a NACC A+ accreditation and is ISO 9001:2015 certified. The Five-Year Integrated MCA programme combines undergraduate and postgraduate education and offers a comprehensive curriculum in programming, software development, data structures, and system design.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination with not less than 45% marks and with Mathematics/Statistics/Accountancy/Informatics Practices as one of the optional subjects

UG Programmes - B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, BCA

SCMS School of Technology and Management is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. SSTM has been awarded a NACC A+ accreditation and is ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring high standards of academic excellence. SSTM offers four-year UG honours programmes in Commerce (with several specialisations), Business Administration, Biotechnology, Psychology, and Computer Applications.

Minimum Eligibility:

B.Com

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination in Commerce Stream with subjects as specified in MGU prospectus
  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination with at least 45% in case of any other stream

BBA

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination in any stream

B.Sc Biotechnology

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination with Biology as an optional subject

B.Sc Psychology

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination any stream

BCA

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination in any stream with Computer Science/ Mathematics/ Statistics/ Accountancy/ Informatics Practices as optional subject

PG Programmes – M.Sc, MCA

SCMS School of Technology and Management is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. SSTM has been awarded a NACC A+ accreditation and is ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring high standards of academic excellence and teaching quality. SSTM offers two-year masters programmes in Computer Applications, Psychology and Molecular Biology & Genetic Engineering.

Minimum Eligibility:

MCA

  • Pass with 50% aggregate in graduation (Reserved category - 45%)
  • Valid rank in Entrance Test conducted by LBS

M.Sc Molecular Biology & Genetic Engineering

  • Graduation in subjects under Biological Sciences as specified in MGU prospectus
  • 50% in Part III subjects (Main/Core + subsidiary/Complementary Courses).

M.Sc Psychology

  • Graduation in Psychology (BA/B.SC) / Rehabilitation Science
  • 50% in Core group (Core + Complementary + Open Courses)

Bachelor of Architecture

The B.Arch programme at SCMS School of Architecture is a 5-year course, approved by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi, and affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. The carefully designed B.Arch curriculum aims to shape future architects by providing comprehensive knowledge and practical skills for a successful profession in architecture.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • Pass in Plus Two/equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects with at least 45% marks in aggregate
  • Valid score in NATA

Bachelor of Technology

SCMS School of Engineering and Technology (SSET) is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by AICTE, accredited A+ by NAAC and NBA. SSET offers 4-year B.Tech programmes in engineering streams including, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Electronics & Communication, VLSI, Mechanical, Automobile, Electrical & Electronics and Civil.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • 45% in Plus Two/equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • Valid Rank in KEAM
  • Valid JEE score accepted for admission under Management quota
  • Entrance Test score not mandatory for admission under NRI Quota

Master of Technology

SCMS School of Engineering and Technology (SSET) is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by AICTE and accredited A+ by NAAC. M.Tech programme in Computer Aided Structural Design (CASE) focuses on advanced techniques in structural engineering and design. M.Tech in Environmental Engineering prepares students to address environmental challenges.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • First class in B.Tech in relevant subjects
  • Candidates having a valid GATE score and a DTE rank will have preference

Master of Computer Applications

SCMS School of Engineering and Technology (SSET) is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by AICTE and accredited A+ by NAAC. The two-year Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programme offered at SSET is designed to provide students with an in-depth knowledge of computer science, programming, and software development.

Minimum Eligibility:

  • Pass with 50% aggregate in graduation (Reserved category - 45%)
  • Valid rank in Entrance Test conducted by LBS
